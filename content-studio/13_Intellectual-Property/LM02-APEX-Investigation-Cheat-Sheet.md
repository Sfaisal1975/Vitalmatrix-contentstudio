## Section 1 — The Three Budget Tiers

### Tier 1 — Essentials (£200–400)
**Purpose:** Target the highest burden zone only. Essential for every patient regardless of budget.
**Focus:** DRD-designated Driver zone. One zone, highest-priority markers.
**Principle:** £200 of targeted testing beats £800 of guessing.

### Tier 2 — Recommended (£400–800)
**Purpose:** Extend to secondary zones and active cascade source zones.
**Focus:** Driver zone + Reactor zone(s) identified in DRD output.
**Principle:** Precision without unnecessary breadth.

### Tier 3 — Comprehensive (£800+)
**Purpose:** Full terrain panel. Every active zone. PRISM cascade-routed.
**Focus:** All zones with composite score above activation threshold, plus cascade source verification.
**Principle:** Complete terrain picture when budget allows.

## Section 2 — Zone-to-Investigation Mapping (Tier 1 Focus)

### Z1 Driver — Metabolic-Energetic Terrain
**Tier 1 markers:** Fasting glucose, HbA1c, full thyroid panel (TSH, fT3, fT4), ferritin, B12, folate, magnesium, mitochondrial stress markers
**Clinical rationale:** Oxidative-metabolic and nutritional-metabolic nodes are the Z1 composition. Investigation targets substrate deficiency and metabolic function.

### Z2 Driver — Gastrointestinal Terrain
**Tier 1 markers:** Calprotectin, comprehensive stool analysis (PCR-based), zonulin/intestinal permeability, IgA/IgG/IgM, CRP, ESR
**Clinical rationale:** Immune-inflammatory and structural-membrane nodes. Investigation targets gut integrity and immune activation.

### Z3 Driver — Cognitive-Neurological Terrain
**Tier 1 markers:** Homocysteine, neurotransmitter precursors (tryptophan, tyrosine), cortisol diurnal profile, heavy metals screen (if exposure history), MTHFR if not already done
**Clinical rationale:** Neuro-endocrine and communication nodes. Investigation targets methylation, neurotransmitter precursors, HPA axis.

### Z4 Driver — Musculoskeletal-Structural Terrain
**Tier 1 markers:** Liver function panel, phase 1/phase 2 detox markers, urine organic acids (detox pathway markers), collagen/connective tissue markers where indicated
**Clinical rationale:** Structural-membrane and detoxification nodes. Investigation targets biotransformation capacity and structural integrity.

### Z5 Driver — Hormonal Terrain Axis (⚠ threshold 32, not 40)
**Tier 1 markers:** Full sex hormone panel (oestrogen, progesterone, testosterone, DHEA, SHBG), cortisol diurnal, insulin, thyroid antibodies (if Z1 also active)
**Clinical rationale:** Neuro-endocrine, nutritional-metabolic, and communication nodes. Z5 activates earlier than other zones — investigation often confirms subclinical dysfunction.

## Section 4 — Investigation Principles

1. **DRD first.** Always identify the highest burden zone before ordering. The Driver zone determines the Tier 1 panel.
2. **Budget is a clinical variable.** Never default to a standard panel. Use the tier that matches the patient's actual budget.
3. **PRISM routing is additive.** Active cascade stacks add markers to the zone-based panel — they do not replace it.
4. **S4 is THEORETICAL.** Z5→Z2 is excluded from DRD trace and investigation routing. Do not order based on S4 alone.
5. **Z5 threshold is 32.** A Z5 score of 34 IS active terrain. Do not wait for 40.
