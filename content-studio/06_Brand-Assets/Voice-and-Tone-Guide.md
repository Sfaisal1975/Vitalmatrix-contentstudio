## Brand Voice in One Sentence

VitalMatrix speaks like a senior functional medicine clinician explaining their methodology to a peer — precise, direct, and uninterested in impressing people who are not the right audience.

---

## The Four Voice Dimensions

### 1. Clinical precision
Use the correct term. Do not simplify to the point of inaccuracy, even for practitioners who may be less familiar with the specific terminology.

| Use | Avoid |
|-----|-------|
| Terrain architecture | Health map |
| Node burden distribution | Where the problem is |
| Cascade propagation | How things are connected |
| Protocol scaffold | Treatment plan template |
| Working terrain architecture | Report / document |
| Clinical intelligence platform | AI tool / smart software |
| Terrain support considerations | Recommendations / advice |

### 2. Peer-to-peer register
VitalMatrix content is always written practitioner-to-practitioner. Not doctor-to-patient. Not company-to-customer. Not expert-to-novice.

The reader is assumed to be a registered functional medicine practitioner with IFM-level training. Do not explain functional medicine to them. Do not define terms they already know.

**Peer-to-peer:** "The NCZ™ node architecture surfaces cascade activity that standard matrix documentation tends to leave implicit."
**Not this:** "VitalMatrix helps you understand how different body systems are connected."

### 3. Understatement over claim
VitalMatrix does not oversell. When in doubt, say less and let the clinical specificity do the work.

**Right:** "VitalMatrix generates working terrain architecture for practitioner review."
**Wrong:** "VitalMatrix revolutionises how you practise functional medicine."
**Wrong:** "VitalMatrix transforms patient outcomes."

### 4. Calm authority
Not urgent. Not anxious. Not needy. VitalMatrix is confident in its clinical value and does not need to shout.

Scarcity (10 founding slots) is stated factually, not dramatised.
Urgency (Phase 1 close date) is stated once, clearly, and not repeated excessively.

## Tone Adjustments by Context

| Context | Tone adjustment | Example |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| Email to warm prospect | Warm, peer-to-peer, unhurried | "I'd like to understand how you work before I tell you anything about VitalMatrix." |
| Discovery call | Conversational clinical peer | Use their language, not VitalMatrix language, when reflecting back |
| Onboarding documents | Clear, professional, instructional | Step-by-step, no jargon beyond what practitioners know |
| LinkedIn articles | Clinical teaching voice | Peer-to-peer, evidence-referenced, no pitch |
| Website copy | Authoritative, precise, slightly formal | Short sentences. No buzzwords. |
| Error / problem handling | Direct, solution-focused | "Contact hello@vitalmatrix.co.uk — mark URGENT if the platform is down." |

---

## Phrases That Are Always Wrong

These must not appear in any VitalMatrix content:

| Never use | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| "AI platform" or "AI-powered" | Regulatory and positioning risk; not our descriptor |
| "diagnoses" / "treats" / "cures" | ASA/MHRA — absolute prohibition |
| "patient outcomes" in a causal frame | Claims outcome without evidence — prohibited |
| "revolutionary" / "transforms" / "game-changing" | Superlative marketing language — not our register |
| "clinical decision support" | Regulatory classification implications |
| "patients" when addressing practitioners | We are B2B. Our audience are practitioners, not patients. |
| "users" | We are a clinical platform. They are practitioners. |

## Phrases That Define the Voice

These are the expressions that, when used correctly, signal VitalMatrix's register:

- "Working terrain architecture"
- "Terrain support considerations"
- "For practitioner review"
- "Node burden distribution"
- "Cascade propagation"
- "The clinical decision remains with you / with the practitioner"
- "Aligned with IFM terrain principles"
- "Clinical intelligence platform"
- "Founding practitioner" / "founding cohort"

## The Standard Disclaimer

Must appear on all content containing T5 clinical claims or platform functionality descriptions:

> *VitalMatrix™ outputs are terrain support considerations only and do not constitute a diagnosis. All clinical decisions remain with the registered practitioner.*

Short form (for space-constrained contexts):
> *VitalMatrix™ outputs are for practitioner review only and do not constitute a diagnosis.*
