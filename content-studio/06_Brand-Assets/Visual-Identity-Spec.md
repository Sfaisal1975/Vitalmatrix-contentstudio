## Logo Usage Rules

- The VitalMatrix™ wordmark always includes the ™ symbol on first reference
- On dark backgrounds: Gold (`#C9A84C`) wordmark on Navy (`#0D2B4E`)
- On light backgrounds: Navy (`#0D2B4E`) wordmark on Off-White (`#F4F4F2`)
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the capital V on all sides
- Do not stretch, rotate, recolour, or apply drop shadows to the wordmark
- "VitalMatrix" is always written as one word, capital V, capital M — never "Vital Matrix" or "vitalmatrix"
