## Interactive Web Tools (email-gated)

1. **Clinical Terrain Quiz** ⭐ PRIORITY — replaces Zone Score Calculator
   12-question clinical assessment based on what the practitioner observes in the patient — symptoms, patterns, treatment response. No node values required. Output: likely active zones, TerrainLock risk flag, suggested investigation tier. Email gate delivers the full terrain pattern summary. See `13_Intellectual-Property/LM03-Clinical-Terrain-Quiz.md` for full question framework.

2. **TerrainLock Risk Checker**
   Symptom-pattern checklist (not zone scores — practitioners do not have zone scores independently). Questions map to the three TerrainLock zones. Email gate for full detection summary and interpretation guide.

3. **APEX Budget Calculator**
   Practitioner selects the presenting zone burden (from clinical picture, not a score) + budget tier. Output: targeted investigation panel for that zone at that tier. Practical, immediate, printable.

4. **Cascade Pattern Identifier**
   Practitioner selects which clinical patterns are present (gut symptoms, energy collapse, hormonal disruption etc). Output: likely active cascade stacks, velocity, clinical narrative. No prior VitalMatrix knowledge required.

5. **DeltaScan Preview Tool**
   *(Phase 2 scope — requires two sets of VitalMatrix outputs to compare. Not a standalone lead magnet.)*

> **Note — Zone Score Calculator removed:** Node values (N1–N7) do not exist independently of VitalMatrix — they are derived from clinical intake within the platform. Asking a practitioner to "enter node values" presupposes they already have access to VitalMatrix. The Clinical Terrain Quiz replaces this concept correctly: it generates a terrain pattern from clinical observation, which is what practitioners actually have.
